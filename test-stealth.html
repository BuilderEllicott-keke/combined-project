How It Works

Stealth Mode Features:
  • Page appears as "about:blank" to external monitoring
  • Title shows "New Tab" when in stealth mode
  • Content is hidden behind a black overlay
  • Shows content on user interaction (click, keypress, mouse movement)
  • Auto-hides after 30 seconds of inactivity
  • Full website functionality available when activated
